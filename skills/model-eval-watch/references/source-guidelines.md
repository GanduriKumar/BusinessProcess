# Source Guidelines

## Preferred Source Types

- Official research blogs and engineering blogs from AI labs
- Official cloud and platform AI blogs when they publish evaluation content
- Reputable open-source ecosystem blogs with evaluation or benchmark material
- Research aggregators and paper feeds when the result remains source-grounded

## Inclusion Rules

- Prefer evaluation-specific material over general product updates.
- Include benchmarks, eval methodology, safety evals, graders, judges, reliability testing, and agent evaluation patterns.
- Keep the watch factual. Do not infer results that are not present in the source.
- Record fetch failures in the report.

## Exclusion Rules

- Do not include generic AI news without a clear evaluation angle.
- Do not include marketing claims that have no visible evaluation content.
- Do not invent summaries beyond what the source supports.
