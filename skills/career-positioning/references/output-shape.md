# Output Shape

## Required Sections

- Senior-role market benchmark
- Common skills, experience, and expectations from recent VP/SVP-level postings
- Positioning summary
- Core market themes
- Post topics
- Asset topics
- Offer ideas
- Suggested publishing sequence

## Offer Quality Bar

Every offer should name:
- target buyer
- business problem
- deliverable
- likely format such as workshop, advisory sprint, or retainer
