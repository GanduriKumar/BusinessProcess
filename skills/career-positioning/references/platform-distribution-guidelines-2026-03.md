# Platform Distribution Guidelines

## LinkedIn

### Official Sources

- LinkedIn Newsletters best practices:
  - https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a517940
- Manage a newsletter on LinkedIn:
  - https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a517925
- Updates to creator mode / follow-first creator features:
  - https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a5999182
- Add or remove a custom button from your profile:
  - https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a1494310

### Supported Guidance

- Keep a clear and consistent topic theme.
- Maintain a visible publishing cadence.
- Use strong headlines for articles and newsletters.
- Add commentary and a question when sharing an article or newsletter into the feed.
- Use cover images for newsletters and articles.
- Optimize SEO title and description for discoverability.
- Use Follow as the primary action when growth is more important than connection gating.
- Use a custom button such as `View my services`, `Book an appointment`, or `View my newsletter`.

### Inference From Official Guidance

- Daily short-form posting is useful only if it reinforces one tight market identity.
- Article and newsletter posts should be used to deepen authority, not to replace feed activity.
- Feed posts should create enough curiosity or relevance that people click `See more`, comment, or move to the profile / newsletter / service CTA.

## Medium

### Official Sources

- Medium Distribution Guidelines:
  - https://help.medium.com/hc/en-us/articles/360006362473
- Partner Program update: rewarding external traffic:
  - https://medium.com/blog/partner-program-update-starting-october-1-were-rewarding-external-traffic-15cb28f75bc9
- Partner Program update: rewarding search traffic:
  - https://medium.com/blog/partner-program-update-starting-november-1-were-rewarding-search-traffic-3ac0635f9f6b

### Supported Guidance

- Write stories that can qualify for broad distribution, not just network distribution.
- Avoid Medium-meta content if the goal is broad reach rather than only reaching existing followers.
- External traffic now matters to monetization.
- Search traffic now matters more to monetization.
- Reader-first, clearly useful, and search-discoverable pieces have structural advantage.

### Inference From Official Guidance

- Medium articles should be evergreen, search-shaped, and externally shareable.
- Titles should be specific and promise real utility rather than vague inspiration.
- Medium works best for durable articles that can rank in search and be shared from LinkedIn or email over time.
