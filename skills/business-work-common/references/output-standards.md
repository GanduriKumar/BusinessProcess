# Output Standards

Use this checklist after drafting any business artifact.

## Audience

- Is the audience clear?
- Does the tone match the audience?
- Is the level of detail right for the audience?

## Evidence

- Are major claims supported by repo data or cited sources?
- Are estimates labeled as estimates?
- Are dates, periods, and units explicit?

## Logic

- Does the conclusion follow from the evidence?
- Are assumptions separated from facts?
- Are risks or dependencies stated when needed?

## Delivery

- Is the opening answer clear?
- Is any section unnecessarily verbose?
- Would an executive understand the output quickly?
