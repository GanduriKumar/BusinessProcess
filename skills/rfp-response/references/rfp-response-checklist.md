# RFP Response Checklist

## Compliance

- Every question answered
- Required format followed
- Mandatory evidence included
- Attachments and references checked

## Writing

- Direct answer first
- Customer language mirrored
- Unsupported claims removed
- Acronyms expanded where needed

## Review

- Evaluation criteria addressed
- Differentiators are factual
- Gaps marked explicitly
- Final pass completed for consistency
