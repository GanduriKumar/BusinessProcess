# Finance Review Workflow

## Objective

Turn financial data into a traceable business answer.

## Steps

1. Clarify the question
- Revenue, cost, margin, pricing, ROI, payback, or variance
- Actual, forecast, or scenario analysis

2. Normalize the data
- Align period, currency, units, and sign conventions
- Reconcile totals to source data

3. Analyze drivers
- Volume, rate, mix, timing, one-time items, and structural changes
- Separate controllable vs external drivers

4. Build the conclusion
- Executive answer
- Key metrics
- Driver explanation
- Risks and assumptions
- Recommendation

5. Review the arithmetic
- Check formulas
- Check totals
- Check narrative against numbers

## Final check

- Every metric has a period and unit
- Actuals and forecasts are not mixed
- Recommendations tie back to math
